## GRIMMER

GRIMMER extends the GRIM test to check whether reported standard deviations are consistent with reported means and sample sizes, assuming integer-valued data. Where GRIM tests the granularity of means, GRIMMER applies analogous mathematical constraints to standard deviations.

## Tools implementing GRIMMER

- [scrutiny](/tools/scrutiny/) — R package
